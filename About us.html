ABOUT US

OVERVIEW

MIT Maharaja Institute of engineering, more fondly known as MIT is situated in the world famous heritage city of Mysuru. It is around 12 Kms from city railway station. MIT was established in the year 1992 by MIT foundation (R) to offer degrees in Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering and Information Science and Engineering. In the year 2000, Mechanical Engineering Program was inducted into to the list of Programs offered.


MIT has a sprawling 25 acres of Land Area amidst prominent industries such as BEML, Infosys etc. Total of 15,282 sqm of built-up area is available for instructional and administrative purposes. In addition,MIT has 26,600 sqm of carpet area for amenities and other utilities. Added attraction is that the institution has a large playground for outdoor sports such as Cricket, Football, Basket Ball and Athletic Track. Two well equipped Hostels with all modern amenities have been constructed to house around 300 students. MIT has well qualified, experienced & dedicated faculty members, which include 12 Ph.D. holders, 28 faculty are pursuing Ph.D., and 25 faculty are master degree holders who are ably supported by experienced technical staff. Currently, there are around 1000 students enrolled.
